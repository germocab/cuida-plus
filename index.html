About us

Care that feels like family

My name is Adriana, and for more than fifteen years I have dedicated myself to providing exceptional care for older adults. I believe that every person — regardless of age or condition — deserves to live with dignity, comfort, and joy.

Every care plan I design is tailored to the unique needs and preferences of each individual. I work closely with families to ensure full transparency and peace of mind every step of the way.

  • Respectful and dignified care in all circumstances
  • Open communication with families at all times
  • Trained, background-checked and fully insured
  • Flexible schedules — part-time, full-time or live-in

What I offer

Services tailored to every stage of life

From light companionship to round-the-clock support, I am here to help with what matters most.

🤝

Companionship & Daily Support

Meaningful conversation, leisure activities, errands, meal preparation and medication reminders — helping seniors stay engaged and independent in their everyday life.

❤️‍🩹

Medical & Health Monitoring

Routine vital sign checks, medication management, appointment coordination and ongoing health tracking with clear family updates and proactive communication.

🛁

Mobility & Personal Hygiene

Respectful and gentle assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming and mobility support — preserving independence while ensuring safety and comfort at all times.

🗓️

Schedules Tailored to You

Every family's situation is unique. Hours, frequency and routines are always discussed together first — so the care plan fits your loved one's life, not the other way around.
